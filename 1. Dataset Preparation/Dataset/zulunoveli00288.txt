 USISHODOLO UYANDIZA

Wakhwela phezu kwezindlu wase egxuma ehlela phansi. 

Wazama ukugxuma esebenzisa ipharashuti. 



 

Emva kwesikhathi wabonakala ekhathele futhi elimele. 

Wathola isu elingcono. Kwakuthi njalo ntambama azilolonge. ' 


 

Abantu bebona lento endizayo bcimangala ukuthi ngabe yini Ie. Bafikelwa ukwesaba okukhulu base bebiza amasosha ukuba azoqapha. 

 

Banquma ukuba lento bayixoshe ngombayimbayi omkhulu. Bhu! Kwaduma umbayimbayi. Inhlamvu enkulu yashweza yadlula eduze kukaSishodolo. 




 

uSishodolo wethuka kakhulu wagwinja wehlela phansi wave wawela phezu kwenqwaba yotshani obuyizithungu. 

Abantu bagijima basondela. Bamangala ukubona ukuthi nguSishodolo lona. 




 


 

Akuthathanga sikhathi abantu bonke babonakala sebendiza, bentweza emoyeni. 

USishodolo wabafundisa abantu ukuthi umshini wokundiza wakhiwa kanjani. Wathola izindodla zemali. 


 

Edolobheni bakha isithombe esiyisifanekiso sikaShishodolo, INTSHE YOKUQALA EYANDIZA.
