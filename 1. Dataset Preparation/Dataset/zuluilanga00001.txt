Mpumuza uMnu David Ntombela, uthe izolo 
ngeSonto ekuseni amalungu ophakathi avuswe 
yisililo siqhuma emzini wakwaPhungula. 

Isidumbu sikaMnu Phungula besidindilizile 
egcekeni lakwakhe eduze kwaso kuhleli lezi zinja 
ezinhlanu ebeziqhubeka nokusidla isidumbu. 
“Kuthe noma amaphoyisa esefikile lezi zinja 
bezisakhombisa inkani, zingafuni ukusuka 
eduze kwesidumbu sikaPhungula. Kuze kwabizwa 
abakwa-SPCA ukuthi kube yibo abazibambayo 
ngoba bezikhombisa inkani enkulu,” kuchaza 
lo mthombo ocele ukuthi lingalidalulwa 
igama lawo ephephandabeni. 

Uqhubeke wathi uMnu Phungula kuthiwa 
ubehambisana noMnu Robert Sithole (68) ongumakhelwane 
wakwakhe, bephelezelana ngezikhathi 
zasekuseni emva kokuziphuzela utshwala 
emzini kaMnu Phungula. UMnu Sithole usinde 
ngokulambisa kulezi zinja kodwa wasala 
namanxeba amabi ezithweni nasezinqeni. 

“Okusindise uSithole kube yijubane lakhe, 
nanokuthi ukwazile ukubalekela emzini wakhe 
wafika wavala isango. UPhungula sengathi yena 
uwe phansi ngesikhathi ethi uyabaleka, zafika 
lezi zinja zamenzakalisa. Izinja ebezinenkani 
kakhulu yilezi zesilisa ebezizokhwela lezi zakwakhe,” 
kusho lo mthombo.

amaphoyisa akule ndawo liwathole esekhona

SPCA.

lezi zinja ezisolwa ngokubulala nokudla isidumbu 
sikaMnu Phungula.
