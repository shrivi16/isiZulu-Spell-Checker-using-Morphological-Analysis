Kanti uthuli nje uNgqeshe usehlulekile ijongosi IikaSitho usejiyelwe icebo ukuthi wenzenjani. Lapho zivalelisa izintombi zikaSitho zixhawula, naye uNgqeshe waxhawula kuMamazane. Lapho ethi uyaphunyula uMamazane, wasibamba wathula naso isandla sentombi uNgqeshe, eyibuka ezinhlamvwini zamehlo. Zasho izinsizwa zakubo,'Ibambe Gqagqalambila, ibambe Silwanesokhuni.' 
UNgqeshe lona isoka lezintombi, uyaziwa kuso sonke isigodi sakubo. Into njena kwakuyinsizwa inobuqha ekhetha izintombi.

Izinsizwa zazimesaba uNg'qeshe ngokuba wayezishayela ensizweni ngenduku, kungelula nokumbangisa ezintombini. Izinsizwana ezazicabanga ngoMamazane zenyela lapho kuzwakala sengathi uNgqeshe uyamfuna.
