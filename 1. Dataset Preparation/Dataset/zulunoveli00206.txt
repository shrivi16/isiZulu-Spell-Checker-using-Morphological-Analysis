 Akekho umuntu okunamelayo ukubizwa ngothathekile. Abantu bangathi unguthathekile, kusho ukuthi ukuthatheka kwakho kunobuwula. Amasiko esintu asemabhadubhadu ngenxa yakho njaIo ukuthatheka. Kube kusaqhamuke abezizwe nezinto zabo, 
sithatheke isintu size sitshinge okwaso, singomothele okwabezizwe. Abamnyama baseNingizimu Afrika babutshengisile lobu buwula ngokungomothela umculo waphesheya kwezilwandle, bayeka owabo wendabuko.
