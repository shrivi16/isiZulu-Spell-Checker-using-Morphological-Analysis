Inkosazana Eyalahlekayo 

Kwesukesukela; 
  Ezweni elithile kwakukhona inkosi eyayinezingane eziningi zabafana namantombazane. Kulezi zingane kwakunentombazanyana yamagcino eyayiyinhle. Inkosi nendlovukazi babeyithanda kakhulu ngaphezu kwazo zonke ezinye izingane zabo. Ezinye izingane zenkosi zakubona lokhu zase zenza iqhinga lokumbulala udadewabo. 
  Ngelinye ilanga abantwana ben kosi bed lola ehlathini babiza umzanyana owayephatha uNtandose (igama lomntwana owayethandwa yinkosi) bathi kuye kukhona omunye wabo owayeselunywe yinyoka. Nempela wabeka uNtandose phansi wagijima eyohlola. Wathola ukuthi babedlala ngaye. Esephindela emuva kuNtandose wamfica engasekho. Abafowabo nodadewabo base bemthathile bayomfihla ehlathini elimnyama, okwakungahambeki kulo futhi lesabeka kakhulu. 
  Umzanyana wamfuna umntwana kwaze kwahlwa engamtholi. "Ntandose! Ntandose! Udliwe yini no? Ntandose, Ntandose!" kwamemeza umzanyana. Akazange athole mpendulo. Waphindela ekhaya wayobikela indlovukazi. Kwaqhuma isililo esikhulu esigodlweni indlovukazi nenkosi bekhala ngomntwana. Umqondo owawukhona kwakungowokuthi uNtandose wabe edliwe isilwane esibi.

Enye inkosikazi eyayizidlulela yamuzwa ekhala. Le nkosikazi yayihlupheka kakhulu. Yayinomntwana nayo womfana owayecishe alingane noNtandose. Ngokuthola intombazanyana enhle, yabe seyikhetha ukubakhulisa bobabili njengabantwana bayo. 

  UNtandose wakhula waba yintombi enhle. Le nkosikazi eyayimtholile yaba nomona ngaye. Yabona ukuthi isu elingcono elokuba agane indodana yayo. Kwakulukhuni lokho ngoba uNtandose wayekhule azi ukuthi ngumfowabo. Inkosikazi yamchazela uNtandose ukuthi wabe ezalwa yinkosi eyayakhe ngaphesheya komfula. Kazange avume ukwendiswa ngaleyo ndlela uNtandose. Waqoma ukuphindela ekhaya ayofuna uyise nonina.
