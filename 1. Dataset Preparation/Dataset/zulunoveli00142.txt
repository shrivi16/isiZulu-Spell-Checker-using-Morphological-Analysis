Kuselawini elikhulu lamazimuzimu, ekuseni ilanga lingakaphumi. Emigibeni kugaxwe amathambothambo (imilenze, izingalo, izimbambo, amakhanda, njalo-njalo), amawisa, imikhonto nezinye izimpahla ezingadingeka zikhona. Amanye amathambo ahlakazeke phansi. Umuntu omkhulu, okhuluphele, ungakwesokunene (kwezibukeli), ukhulekiwe ukuze angakwazi ukubaleka. lziko elikhulu liphakathi nendlu, linomlilo ongasavuthi ngamandla. Kulo kubekwe imbiza enkulu. Imigodla nezingogo kuningi, kutakwe ngakwesokunxele ukuya ngasemsamo kancane.
