ISIBINGELELO 

Abanye phakathi kwethu sebezwile ukuthi inkambo yethongo 
elikhulu lesizwe sakwaZulu isicishe yadlulelwa yisikhathi. 
Inkambo kaShaka yabhalwa ngeminyaka edlule ngesiXhosa, 
kwabuye kwathi uMofolo wabhala ethi Chaka ngesiSuthu, 
okuyiyona ncwadi evelele ezincwadini zesiSuthu. Manje le 
ncwadi isize yahunyushwa ngesiNgisi. Kodwa kakukho ncwadi 
yesiZulu ephethe inkambo kaShaka. Phakathi kwezizathu 
mhlawumbe yingoba kakukho kwaZulu obe nesibindi sokugcoba 
phansi isimo somuntu owenza izigigaba ezingaka endabukweni 
yamaZulu. Isithunzi sikaShaka sikhulu kangangoba igama lakhe 
selinjengelamaqhawe ezinsumansumane ngenxa yokuhlabana 
kwakhe. 

UDhlomo-ke lo uzethwese lo msebenzi wabheka phambili. 
Akalingi kudlalisela ngomsebenzi wakhe njengoMofolo. Yena 
usuke wagcoba phansi indabuko yendaba kaShaka njengoba 
injalo, ecobelela ukwazi kwakhe kubantu nakubeLungu. 
Amaxoxo awasusa emicabangweni akhona kule ncwadi, nokho, 
ayayelula incwadi, ayinandise. Lapho umuntu eyifunda yonke 
incwadi uzwa uzwelo olusemphefumulweni, nokumangaliswa 
yileli qhawe lakwaZulu. UDhlomo ukholwa ukuthi uNandi 
wazifela, akavumelani namahemuhemu athi uShaka wambulala.

AMAZWANA OMLOBI WALA MAXOXO 

Lokho ngikwenze, njengoba sengishilo, ukuze indaba ihambe 
igijime, kesizwe nabo bekhuluma ngeyabo imilomo. Noma 
ngizame ngezindlela zonke ukufunda ngisho nokungangenhloko 
yesipenetu ngoShaka, ngiyaqinisa ngithi kuningi okusasele ngaye, 
kodwa kangisho ukuthi okumqoka nesivame ukukuzwa ngaye 
ngikushiyile. 

Into ebe lukhuni kimina yikuba ngilandelise kahle konke 
ukuhamba kukaShaka impela, kusuka ekuzalweni kwakhe 
kuze kuyofika ekufeni kwakhe. Lokho kwenziwe ukuba 
ngingawutholanga umthombo obungangisiza kulokho, ngaseke 
ngimelwa ukuthatha lapha ngithathe laphaya, ngihlanganise 
ngenze indaba ezwakalayo. 

Noma-ke ngikwenzile lokho, kangisho neze ukuthi 
kuzoyithikameza le ndaba kuyenze ingafundeki, noma phela 
ifundeke ngobudluthudluthu. Qha, ngithi izofundeka kahle nje, 
ingalumeli ngaleso sizathu.
