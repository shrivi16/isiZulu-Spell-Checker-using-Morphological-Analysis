(b) UMDLALO WOMSAKAZO 
Lona ngumdlalo obhalelwe inzwa yokulalela, indlebe phela. 
Sinele sizwe ngendlebe lokho esikuxoxelwayo, bese sizakhela thina izithombe zakho ngamehlo engqondo. Lolu hlobo lomdlalo lusuke lucupheke kahle ekubhalweni kwalo uma sikwazi ukwahlukanisa imisindo emdlalweni. Phakathi kwakho singabala ukuhoza kwempophoma nokugeleza kwamanzi. Ukushayisana kwezimoto nokugingqika kwemoto. Umsindo wezinyekevu nomsindo wezihlonono. Lo mdlalo-ke wona uncike engxoxweni kakhulu ngenxa yokuthi umlaleli akakwazi ukuzibonela kwenzeka phambi kwakhe lokho wena mbhali ozakhela khona ngamehlo engqondo khathi usabhala. Nayo njalo ingxoxo eyethulwayo ingathemelezi belu, ,kodwa iwukhulise umdlalo ngokushuba kwesimo. Amagama owasebenzisayo wonge kakhulu ukuze umdlalo wakho ungabi yisicefe nendavundavu nje kumlaleli. Umdlalo olalelwayo akumele neze ukuba ugide ndawonye. Kubaluleke kakhulu ukuba ube nokuxhumana okusuka kwelinye igxathu kuqonge kuze kwenyukele kwelinye iqophelo. Izithombemagama ziyasiza futhi ekunothiseni ulimi. Kubaluleke kakhulu ukuba umlaleli umhehe, umbambe ngqi ngendlela inkulumo oyibeka ngayo, nang end lela amaphuzu akho owalandelanise ngayo, ukuze angazitholi esethinte inkinobho yomsakazo wakhe waguqukela kwesinye isiteshi somsakazo.
