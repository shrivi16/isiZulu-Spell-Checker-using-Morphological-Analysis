   Ngithanda ukulazisa lonke ukuba indaba ekhulunywa kule incwadi yindaba ephuma ekhanda lami ngelizwe lakithi. Amabizo wonke ngawami engizidalele wan a, kodwa angamabizo esiNtu ajayelekileyo njalo ayaziwa ngabantu kakhulu phakathi kwethu. Labo-ke abalamabizo afanana lawo bayanxuswa ukuba bangaze badubeka emiphefumulweni yabo ngoba akuqondiswa kibo. 
   Ngiyalicela ukuba libe lesineke uma Iifunda indaba ka.vusezindala. Ungeze wayizwisisisa kuhle ngaphandle kokuthi uzwe ezinye izindaba engizifake phansi; ngoba lanxa kungathi zahlukene kodwa zibumba indaba eyodwa. Zona zingamaseko asekela indaba. 
   Njalo phakathi kwendaba Ie lizatica kulamabizo amanengi athintwa kwezinye izindawo angaphindi avele futhi. Lokho kwenziwa yikubana indaba ifingqiwe ukuze ibe yindaba emfitshane. Kube nzima kimi ukuba ngiwalobe wonke ngoba angathatha indawo enkulu.
