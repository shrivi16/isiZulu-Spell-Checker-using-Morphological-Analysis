KUCHAZWA AMAGAMA 
Bandla la kwethu, ngivumeleni bakwethu nginihlomulise ngengxe nyana yokuchaza lawo magama angani nikeza ubunzima lapho nise mshikashikeni wokufunda izinkondlo. Ngalo rnzamo nginethemba futhi nginesiqiniseko sokuthi niyosizakala futhi nicebe olimini kubuye kunibeke ethubeni lokushesha ukuthola umqondo wenkondlo noma kuthiwa ucashile: 
  Ngawo 10 rnzamo nami ngizamile ukwenyula lawo magama, ngawachaza. Nizokuqaphela futhi ekuhamba hambeni nezosha uku. thi kukhona lawo magama ayekiwe nje angachazwa. Ningethukike bese nigangatheka nithi, hhiya sase sihlatshwa ngomkhonto owodwa nje kule ndlala kaMadlantule, sekwenze njani? 
  Qha, lawo magama ayobe eyekwe ngabomu ukuze phela sonke sisebenze ngokuzikhandla. Niyakhuthazwa.ke ukuthi nxa unqwa. mana nalawo magama ungabe usuwashalazela uphike ngokuthi vele alukhuni, kodwa bheka lelo gama kwizichazamazwi zesiZulu . Ngokwenzenjalo uyobe uzibeka ethubeni elihle lokwandisa ulwazi Iwakho. 
Phuzani-ke, lona ngumthombo oyilifa lenu laphakade, imfundo! 
Ningalubukeli phansi ulimi Iwenu IwesiZulu ngoba yilona oluyo. buyisa isithunzi nokuhlonipheka kwenu.
